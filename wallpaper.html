What I Work With

ALL WALLPAPER
TYPES COVERED

From classic paste-the-wall to modern peel & stick — I handle every type with the same precision and care.

Non-Pasted Wallpaper

Traditional wallpaper that requires paste applied separately. Offers the widest selection of designs and materials. I mix the paste to the right consistency and apply evenly for a lasting, smooth finish.

Most durable
Pre-Pasted Wallpaper

Comes with a dry adhesive backing — just activate with water. Easier to work with but still requires skill for perfect seams and pattern alignment. Quick installation with a clean result.

Faster install
Vinyl Wallpaper

Durable, moisture-resistant, and easy to clean — ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas. Vinyl wallpaper requires careful surface prep and strong adhesive for a long-lasting bond.

Best for wet areas
Peel & Stick Wallpaper

Self-adhesive and removable — perfect for renters or accent walls. Trickier than it looks to install without bubbles or crooked seams. I get it smooth, straight, and perfectly aligned every time.

Renter-friendly
